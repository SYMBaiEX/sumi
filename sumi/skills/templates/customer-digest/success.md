# Template: Customer Digest — Success

A periodic digest for a customer success audience. Expansion signal, churn risk, and relationship health across the book of business. Pulls from Gmail (customer threads), Calendar (customer meetings), and Slack (customer channels).

## Structure

```
*{{display_name}} — {{start_date}} → {{end_date}}*

*TL;DR*
{{One sentence on the state of the book this period.}}

*📈 Expansion signal*
• {{Account. Signal (new use case, referral, exec intro). Next step.}}

*🚨 Churn risk*
• {{Account. Signal (negative NPS, missed usage, escalation). Mitigation plan.}}

*🗓️ Notable customer meetings*  ← only if Calendar is a source
• {{Meeting. Account. Outcome / next step.}}

*🏆 Wins worth amplifying*
• {{Account success worth case-studying or telling the team about.}}

*📣 Quotes from customers*
• "{{quote}}" — Account.

*🙋 Asks*
• {{Decision, intro, hand-off CS needs from another team.}}

*🔜 Follow-ups this period*
• {{Account — specific follow-up the CSM owns.}}
```

## Rules

- **Attribute to the account, not the individual** unless the user has specified otherwise.
- **Rank by $ or strategic weight** — top accounts first, not alphabetical.
- **Every risk needs a plan.** If there's no plan, that's the ask — escalate it.
- **Separate signal from noise.** A single negative email isn't churn risk — a pattern is.
