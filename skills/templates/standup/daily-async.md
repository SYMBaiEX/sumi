# Template: Standup — Daily Async

A short async standup post. Three sections: yesterday, today, blockers. First-person. Posts to a standup channel or DM. Meant to be read by teammates in under 30 seconds.

## Structure

```
*{{display_name}} — {{start_date}}*

*Yesterday*
• {{What I finished or made meaningful progress on.}}

*Today*
• {{What I'm picking up — 1 to 3 things max.}}

*Blockers*
• {{What's stopping me. Who can help. If nothing, "_None_".}}
```

## Rules

- **3 bullets per section max.** This is a standup, not a status report.
- **Name people when asking for help.** "@Sam, can you review the migration PR?" is useful; "need review" is not.
- **No context section.** If you need more than three bullets of context, schedule a sync.
- **Write it for teammates**, not for your manager. Direct, not polished.
