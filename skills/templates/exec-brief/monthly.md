# Template: Exec Brief — Monthly

Monthly roll-up for an exec / board audience. Wider window (30 days) so include trend context, not just the last week. Still terse — execs don't become more patient on a longer cadence.

## Structure

```
*{{display_name}} — {{start_date}} → {{end_date}}*

*TL;DR*
{{One or two sentences. What happened this month and what it means.}}

*📈 Metrics (month-over-month)*
• {{Metric — M/M Δ, absolute, trend (3-month direction).}}

*🏁 Milestones hit / missed*
• {{Milestone. Status: hit / slipped / moved. One-line consequence if missed.}}

*✅ Major decisions*
• {{Decision. Date. Owner. Why it mattered.}}

*🚩 Risks trending up*
• {{Risk. Change since last month. Plan.}}

*🔍 Spotlight*
• {{One thing worth a paragraph — a key launch, a customer dynamic, a macro factor.}}

*🙋 Needed from the exec*
• {{Decision, intro, hire approval, spend approval. Ideally one.}}

*🔜 Next month priorities*
• {{Top 3 priorities for the coming month.}}
```

## Rules

- **Trend context required.** One datapoint is useless at this cadence — show direction.
- **Misses acknowledged.** Missed milestones must be named; execs always notice their absence.
- **Spotlight has prose.** The one exception to "no narrative" — a short paragraph where the signal needs context.
- **Cap at one page equivalent.** ~300 words total. If it's longer, trim.
